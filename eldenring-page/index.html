Tarnished

The world of Elden Ring is the Lands Between, once ruled by order maintained through the Elden Ring. When the Ring was shattered, Queen Marika’s demigod children went to war over its fragments. The player is a Tarnished, an exile called back by the power of Grace, whose goal is to gather the Great Runes, defeat the demigods, restore the Ring, and become the Elden Lord. In the end, the player chooses the world's fate—whether to restore order, burn it all in chaos, or begin a new era beyond the gods’ control.

Useful Items

Somber Smithing Stone: A rare and dark-forged mineral used to enhance special armaments of legendary origin. Unlike ordinary smithing stones, it strengthens unique weapons one tier at a time, each infusion drawing forth deeper, hidden power slumbering within the blade

Smithing Stone: A common yet essential mineral used to reinforce standard weapons and shields. With each tier, it sharpens steel and strengthens spirit, allowing ordinary arms to stand against even the mightiest foes.

Whetstone Knives: Mystical blades used by smiths and warriors to etch ancient techniques into their weapons. Each variant unlocks new affinities—be it flame, magic, or poison—allowing arms to be tailored to a warrior's chosen path. From the Iron Whetblade to the Black Whetblade, these sacred tools bind weapon and will.

MAIN STORY BOSSES

Radahn

Starscourge Radahn: A legendary demigod and master of gravity magic, who halted the stars themselves—now a fallen champion, driven mad amidst the sands of Caelid

Godfrey

Godfrey, First Elden Lord: The first champion of the Golden Order, a mighty warrior who cast aside his grace and became a brutal exile—now returned to test the worth of those who would claim the throne

Malenia

Malenia, Blade of Miquella: A peerless swordswoman cursed with Scarlet Rot, she has never known defeat—her grace and fury entwined in a dance of death beneath her unyielding will.

Renalla

Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon: A sorceress of unparalleled power and head of Raya Lucaria, she wields moonlight magic and guards the secrets of rebirth within her shattered heart.

Rykard

Rykard, Lord of Blasphemy: Once a noble demigod, he fed his body to the great serpent and became a monstrous god-devouring horror, seeking strength through defiance of the Erdtree.

Radagon

Radagon of the Golden Order: A god in radiant form, once consort to both Rennala and Marika, he stands as the final guardian of the Golden Order—his shattered self bound to divine purpose.
